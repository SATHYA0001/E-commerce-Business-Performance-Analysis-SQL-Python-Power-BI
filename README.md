# E-commerce-Business-Performance-Analysis-SQL-Python-Power-BI

## Problem Statement

Olist is a Brazilian e-commerce marketplace connecting 
small businesses to customers nationwide. This analysis 
investigates 100,000+ orders across 2016–2018 to answer:

"How can Olist leverage its data to identify revenue trends, 
reduce churn, improve satisfaction, and drive growth?"

## Key Business Questions
1. What is the monthly revenue trend?
2. Which product categories drive the most revenue?
3. Which geographies are most valuable?
4. Where is the order funnel breaking down?
5. What is driving low customer satisfaction?
6. Who are our most valuable and at-risk customers?
